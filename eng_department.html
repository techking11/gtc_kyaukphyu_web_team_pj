About Our Department

The English Department is one of the supporting departments at the Government Technological College (Kyaukphyu). The English Department has been creating a stimulating learning environment for students. Students' achievement, improvement and competency in English Language are our main goals. We develop studentsâ€™ existing knowledge about English Language and broaden their horizon in using English in scientific and engineering contexts.

Our Mission

  1. To enable students to become proficient in and use English
  2. To become fluent in English
  3. To become English proficient and outstanding students not only in the classroom but also outside the classroom

Our Vision

  1. To make students interested in and proficient in English
  2. To enable students to have good English skills and good understanding of textbooks
  3. To practice speaking more in the classroom to improve students' speaking skills
  4. To organize English skill competitions and improve the English skills of students
