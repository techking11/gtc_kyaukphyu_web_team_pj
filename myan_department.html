About Our Department

Myanmar subject is one of the academic subjects for first year students. Since it is the mother language, it is essential to teach Myanmar subject in arts and science universities including technological universities. Myanmar subject is very broad and there is no ending to learn the subject, Myanmar. By teaching Myanmar subject, students are aimed to become patriots, to be skillful at using Myanmar language and to love their own culture.

Our Mission

  1. To know and understand the basics of Myanmar writing
  2. To know and understand basic Myanmar social ethics
  3. To be able to write Myanmar language effectively and accurately
  4. To get the basics in writing technical literature
  5. To get the basic knowledge of translation
  6. To improve the attitudes and ethics of the people

Our Vision

  1. To fulfill the key requirements to improve communication and writing skills for the benefit of the people through technology and communication in the workplace.
