Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to apply for admission to the Government Industrial Institute (Kyauk Phyu) ?

The information to be brought when applying for admission to Government Industrial Institute (Kyauk Phyu).

  1. Matriculation mark sheet original
  2. Original letter of recommendation from the village/ward administrator of good character
  3. Township police endorsement original
  4. Original medical certificate of good health
  5. Copy of both student's and parent's registration; Default (direct filter)
  6. A copy of the household chart with the student
  7. (3) passport photos
What are the enrollment requirements for first-year students?

The following are the requirements for students who have been admitted to the first year.

  1. 3 photos (red hair/bad color (female)/long hair (male) are not included in the photo)
  2. scorecard (original)
  3. Copy of Class 10 Certificate (Bring Original)
  4. Registration (original and copy)
  5. Parental Register (original and copy)
  6. Ward support letter
First year second year What are the enrollment requirements for third year (pass/fail) students ?

First year second year The following are the documents required for third year (pass/fail) students to enroll.

  1. Copy of school/parent registration
  2. Three (3) photos (photos must be taken in school uniform. Red/dark hair (male/female) and long hair (male) are not allowed.)
What do I need to enroll in GTHS Specials ?

The documents required for enrollment in GTHS special education are as follows.

  1. scorecard (original)
  2. Copy of GTHS certificate (to bring original)
  3. 3 photos (photos must be taken in school uniform. Red hair/dark colored (gender) and long hair (gender) are not allowed.)
  4. Copy of student/parent registration
