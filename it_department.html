About Our Department

To develop large scale software that powers business and industry, computer scientists use their technical and creative skills to improve people’s lives in almost every area imaginable. The complex software and hardware systems created by computer scientists impact all aspects of society and influence or transform work done in areas as diverse as medicine, education, music, travel and business.

Our Mission

  1. Systematic training with practical experience to become knowledgeable IT engineers
  2. Launching short-term training courses to produce skilled developers
  3. Visiting foreign-invested companies to study job requirements

Our Vision

  1. To produce knowledgeable IT engineers
  2. To be able to supply enough skilled developers for the region
  3. To provide job opportunities to work in foreign-invested companies
