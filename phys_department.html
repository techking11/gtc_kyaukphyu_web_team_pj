About Our Department

The Department of Engineering Physics was established In Government Technological College (Kyaukphyu) since 1995. Currently, it offers as pre-engineering course to a bachelor degree of Engineering taught for one year. All first year students are admitted every year and they study theoretical concepts as well as practical of Engineering Physics.

Our Mission

  1. To become qualified, outstanding and practical experts

Our Vision

  1. To become the talented engineers needed for the country
