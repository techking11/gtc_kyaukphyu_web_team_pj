About Our Department

Chemistry is important for engineers to have knowledge of chemistry as those may face problems in fields as diverse as design and development of new materials, quality control and environmental engineering that are basically chemistry oriented in nature. It is the back bone in designing and understanding the nature of various engineering materials, Many advances in engineering and technology either produce a chemical demand like polymers, chemical developments for their application in powder metallurgy and alloys, preventing methods of pollution etc.

Our Mission

  1. To make students understand and enjoy the training of the Department of Engineering and Chemistry
  2. To teach the lessons in a practical way

Our Vision

  1. To assist in the development of qualified engineering human resources for the country with the help of engineering chemistry concepts.
