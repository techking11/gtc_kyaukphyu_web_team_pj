Admission Requirements

The following information must be submitted with the application

  1. Bring 1 copy and original student's NRC Card and student's parents' NRC Cards
  2. If you are still applying for a National Identity Card, please bring letter of recommendation (original) from the Ministry of Immigration and Population that you are still applying.
  3. Bring letter of recommendation from police station.
  4. Bring medical check report from local health department.
  5. Bring copy of Family Registration Sheets (or) Census Registration Sheet (must be brought in original for verification).
  6. Bring copy/original of matriculation certificate.
  7. Bring score list of matriculation examination (original).
  8. Bring 10 passport size color photos taken within 1 month.
