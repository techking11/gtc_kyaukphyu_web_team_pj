About Our Department

Engineering Mathematics Department is one of the most popular department in Technological University of Hmawbi. Mission of the department is to educate and train the students to be able to perform effectively in real world, to become ICT professionals in various ICT fields.

Our Mission

  1. Based on mathematical knowledge and experience, to systematically solve the challenges of the modern age and develop highly capable human resources.

Our Vision

  1. To be able to provide effective support in producing good engineers who are brilliant, thoughtful, ethical and ethical.
