About Our Department

Department of electrical power engineering is one of the main engineering departments of Government Technological College (Kyaukphyu). Department of Electrical power engineering provides an Electrical engineering education to under graduates (Bachelor of Engineering )and post graduate students (Master of Engineering) . This Department has been established since thirty years ago.

Our Mission

  1. To give every citizen access to industrial and vocational education and job opportunities
  2. To produce qualified and skilled workers
  3. To provide students with work experience and opportunities
  4. To be able to open different types of teaching and learning training according to local needs
  5. To provide training opportunities for teachers to produce the necessary skills for the workplace

Our Vision

  1. To make students more knowledgeable and practical
  2. To be able to use electricity systematically and professionally in the workplace
  3. To prevent workplace hazards
  4. To be able to use electronic devices in their place
  5. To build a modern and developed nation with electricity
